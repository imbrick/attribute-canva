Installation

To install the Attributes Canva theme:

  • Go to your WordPress dashboard and navigate to Appearance > Themes.
  • Click on Add New, then Upload Theme.
  • Choose the attributes-canva.zip file and click Install Now.
  • Activate the theme after installation.

You can now begin customizing your site!

Starter Content

When you activate the theme on a fresh WordPress install, starter content will be automatically added. This includes:

  • A homepage with a welcome message.
  • An "About Us" page to share your story.
  • A "Blog" page for your posts.
  • A "Contact" page with placeholder text for contact details.

You can customize or delete this content from the WordPress editor.

Using the Customizer

The Customizer allows you to change your site's appearance in real-time. To access the Customizer:

  • Go to Appearance > Customize in your WordPress dashboard.

The Customizer includes settings for:

  • Typography: Choose a font for your site's body text.
  • Colors: Adjust the accent color to match your brand.
  • Menus: Manage and assign navigation menus.

Demo Content Import

If you want your site to look exactly like the demo, follow these steps:

  1. Install and activate the One Click Demo Import plugin.
  2. Go to Appearance > Import Demo Data.
  3. Select the demo content and click Import.

Note: Ensure all recommended plugins are installed before importing demo content.

Using the Contact Widget

The Contact Widget allows you to display contact information in your sidebar or footer. To use it:

  • Go to Appearance > Widgets.
  • Drag and drop the Contact Info widget into your desired widget area.
  • Fill out the title and contact details, then click Save.

Getting Support

If you need help, you can:

  • Check the Theme Docs in your WordPress admin menu.
  • Visit our support page.
